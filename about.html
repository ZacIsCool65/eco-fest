About the Festival

Welcome to a celebration where timeless music meets a vision for a more sustainable future.

Our Eco Festival Classic brings together the elegance of classical music and the spirit of environmental awareness in a unique open-air experience. Set against a backdrop of nature, the festival invites audiences to reconnect with both cultural heritage and the natural world.

Beyond the performances, the festival is committed to sustainability at every level. From low-impact staging and renewable energy use to zero-waste initiatives and locally sourced food vendors, every detail is designed with the environment in mind.

Join us for an unforgettable experience where art, history, and ecology come together in harmony.
